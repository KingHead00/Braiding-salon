Our Story

Braiding is Our Crown
& Culture

Founded with a deep reverence for the art of African braiding, Queen of Braid Studio is where tradition breathes and creativity soars. Each braid is a conversation — between our hands and your hair, between heritage and modernity.

We believe protective styling should never compromise your edges, your time, or your joy. Every client leaves not just with beautiful hair, but feeling truly seen and celebrated.

  • ✦ Premium, hair-safe products exclusively
  • ✦ Personalized consultations for every style
  • ✦ Clean, welcoming, calming studio space
  • ✦ Committed to edge-preserving techniques
